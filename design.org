* Scripture Memorizer Design
In here I will place my design artifacts and work for the Scripture Memorizer
app.

I have been using Copilot as a guide to help me navigate through the design
process.

** Persona
Name: Scott
Age: 32
Loves his family.
Wants to memorize scriptures to strengthen his relationship with his Father in
Heaven and to clarify his spiritual motivations.

** Promise (Value Proposition)
This app enables you to practice recalling scriptures and gives you clear,
honest and specific feedback on what you miss, so you can build confidence.

** Golden Flow
This is a description of the happy path for the persona.  This should summarize
their motivations and what he/she or the app does to accomplish them.

"5-Minute Recall Session"
*** 1. Session Entry
- Scott opens the app and immediately sees the scripture reference he is
  currently practicing.
- Scott indicates that he is ready to begin reciting.

*** 2. Recitation Attempt
- The app makes it clear that it is listening.
- Scott recites the scripture (or a portion of it) from memory.
- Scott indicates that he is finished with this attempt.

*** 3. Feedback and Decision
- The app shows precisely which words were correct and which were missed, in
  context.
- The app makes Scott's current level of recall clear.
- Scott chooses to either:
  - Attempt the same portion again
  - End the session for now

** Design Anchors
If any of these moments fail, the app has failed to deliver its promise.

- Immediate recognition of what Scott is memorizing.
- Clarity that the app is listening.
- Precision of feedback.
